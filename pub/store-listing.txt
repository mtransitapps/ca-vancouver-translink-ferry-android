Full description:
This app adds Vancouver ferries information to MonTransit.

This app provides the ferry schedule and news from www.translink.ca, YouTube and @TransLink and @TransLinkMedia on Twitter.

Vancouver ferries serve Vancouver and the North Shore in British Columbia, Canada.

Once this application is installed, the MonTransit app will display ferry information (schedule...).

This application only has a temporary icon and no screen: download the MonTransit app (free) in the "More ..." section bellow or by following this Google Play link http://goo.gl/pCk5mV

You can install this application on the SD card but it is not recommended.

The information comes from the GTFS file provided by TransLink.
https://developer.translink.ca/ServicesGtfs/GtfsData

This application is free and open-source:
https://github.com/mtransitapps/ca-vancouver-translink-ferry-android

This app is not related with TransLink (South Coast British Columbia Transportation Authority).

Permissions:
- Other: required to read news from www.translink.ca, YouTube and Twitter
